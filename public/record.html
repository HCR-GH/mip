Record your own memory

Press the record button to start your recording and the stop button when you are done. You can listen back to your recording in the audio player below. If you'd like to re-do your recording simply press record again and your new recording will replace your previous one. Don't forget to allow microphone permissions if prompted.

If you'd like to save your recording use the button below to download it to your device
